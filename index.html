Rules

  • Target is a random number.
  • Win by making Score equal Target.
  • Increase Score by clicking on a gem.
  • Each gem has a random value which stays the same until a win or a loss.
  • After a win or a loss all gems have a new value.
  • Lose if Score is greater than Target.
